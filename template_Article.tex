\begin{center}

\begin{center} $E_{A} (e) = |e_{4}-e|$ \\

\begin{center}

	= $1 + 1 + \displaystyle\frac1{2} + \displaystyle\frac1{6} + \displaystyle\frac1{24} = e_{4} = 2.7083$
\end{center}
\begin{center} $E_{A} (e) = |2,7083-e|   / e = proporcionado por Octave $ \\
